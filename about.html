We Are

Slania is a symphonic metal band from Buenos Aires founded in september of 2010 by Mauricio Rodriguez, Florencia Sategna and Marianela Valdata. Nowadays it is integrated by Florencia Sategna on female vocals, Walter Gonzalez on bass and male vocals, Gonzalo Kobalenko on guitars, Sebastian Guerrero on keyboards and Fernando Romeo Ortiz on drums.

The band gives its first steps towards early 2011 and with the incorporation of the other members, it starts the composition of its first material.

Focusing on the search of their own sound, without departing from symphonic, Slania includes influences of other subgenres in its material such as gothic, progressive and melodic death metal.

After many shows and several line-up changes, Slania manages the development of its live sound. Finally, the band consolidates its proposal and enters the studio in late 2015 to record its fist album. Entitled 'Proof of Existence', their album is released on October 15th.
